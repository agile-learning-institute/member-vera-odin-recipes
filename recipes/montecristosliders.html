These warm Monte Cristo Sliders have all the classic flavors you love! Turkey, ham and Swiss cheese all packed into Hawaiian sweet rolls and topped with a buttery spread!

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg 12-count each Sweet Hawaiian Dinner Rolls
  • 1/2 pound deli-sliced smoked or honey ham
  • 1/2 pound deli-sliced smoke roasted turkey
  • 1/2 pound Gruyere cheese or other Swiss cheese, grated

For the sauce:

  • 1 stick butter, melted
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons Duke's mayonnaise

For the garnish:

  • Powdered sugar
  • Red Raspberry Preserves

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Line a 9x13 baking dish with foil (enough that it overlaps the sides - to use as handles.)
  3. Spray foil-lined baking dish with cooking spray.
  4. Slice rolls horizontally as a unit - don't break apart into individual rolls.
  5. Put the bottoms into the baking dish.
  6. Lay meat on top, then cover with cheese.
  7. In a small bowl, whisk together sauce ingredients
  8. Pour 1/2 of the sauce on top of the cheese.
  9. Place tops of rolls on top, pressing down lightly.
  10. Pour remaining mixture over tops of rolls and spread evenly.
  11. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes.
  12. Watch closely - if the tops begin to brown too much, place a sheet of foil over the top.
  13. Remove from oven and let cool for about 10 minutes.
  14. Using the foil "handles" lift the sliders out onto a cutting board.
  15. With large butcher knife, cut the sandwiches into their individual slider rolls.
  16. Dust generously with powdered sugar and serve with raspberry preserves on the side.
